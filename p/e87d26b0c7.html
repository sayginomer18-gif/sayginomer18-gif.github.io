My Şavşat Experience: Discovering Wetlands

If someone told me a year ago that Şavşat would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

The wetlands scene in Şavşat is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

The infrastructure around wetlands in Şavşat is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

I documented my entire wetlands experience in Şavşat through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.

As I left Şavşat, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
