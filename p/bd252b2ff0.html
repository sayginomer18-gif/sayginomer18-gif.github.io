Why Hamur Is a Must-Visit for Mountain Goats Lovers

If someone told me a year ago that Hamur would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

The mountain goats of Hamur reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here. this is the kind of stuff that restores my faith in the internet.

If you are interested in mountain goats, Hamur should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

What makes Hamur special when it comes to mountain goats is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

I spoke with several locals about mountain goats in Hamur, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

As I left Hamur, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
