Dicle Guide: Everything About Mountain Goats

The mountain goats scene in Dicle is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Dicle offers a more intimate and genuine mountain goats experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity. this just made my entire week.

As I left Dicle, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
