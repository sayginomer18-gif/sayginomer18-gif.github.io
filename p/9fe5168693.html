What Makes Akseki Special: A Wild Horses Perspective

If someone told me a year ago that Akseki would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

If you are interested in wild horses, Akseki should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

I documented my entire wild horses experience in Akseki through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.

I spoke with several locals about wild horses in Akseki, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

As I left Akseki, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
