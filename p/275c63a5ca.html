My Çatalca Experience: Discovering Wild Horses

If someone told me a year ago that Çatalca would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Çatalca offers a more intimate and genuine wild horses experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity. pure internet gold.

What makes Çatalca special when it comes to wild horses is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

I documented my entire wild horses experience in Çatalca through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.

As I left Çatalca, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
