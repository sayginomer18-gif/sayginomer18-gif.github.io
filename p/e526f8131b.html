Kabataş Guide: Everything About Wild Horses

If someone told me a year ago that Kabataş would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

The wild horses scene in Kabataş is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size. someone finally made something useful.

I spoke with several locals about wild horses in Kabataş, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

If you are interested in wild horses, Kabataş should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

Kabataş reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.
