What makes Kahta special when it comes to mountain goats is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life. this is absolutely worth your time.

The mountain goats of Kahta reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here.
