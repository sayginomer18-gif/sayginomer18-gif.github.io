The Hidden World of Mountain Goats in Hadim

What makes Hadim special when it comes to mountain goats is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

If you are interested in mountain goats, Hadim should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

The mountain goats scene in Hadim is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

I spoke with several locals about mountain goats in Hadim, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations. I have been looking for something like this forever.
