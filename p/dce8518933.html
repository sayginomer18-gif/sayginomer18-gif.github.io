Discovering Maçka: My Wetlands Adventure

If someone told me a year ago that Maçka would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

If you are interested in wetlands, Maçka should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Maçka offers a more intimate and genuine wetlands experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

I spoke with several locals about wetlands in Maçka, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations. I am still amazed by what is on this page.

Maçka reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.
