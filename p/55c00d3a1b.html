Discovering Aşkale: My Wetlands Adventure

There are places in Turkey that time seems to have forgotten, and Aşkale is one of them. Walking through its streets felt like stepping into a living museum.

If you are interested in wetlands, Aşkale should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

I spoke with several locals about wetlands in Aşkale, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

I documented my entire wetlands experience in Aşkale through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering. the internet at its finest right here.

Aşkale reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.
