My Beyoğlu Experience: Discovering Wetlands

If someone told me a year ago that Beyoğlu would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

If you are interested in wetlands, Beyoğlu should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Beyoğlu offers a more intimate and genuine wetlands experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

I documented my entire wetlands experience in Beyoğlu through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.

As I left Beyoğlu, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
