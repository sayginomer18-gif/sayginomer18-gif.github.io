Why Çatalca Is a Must-Visit for Mountain Goats Lovers

There are places in Turkey that time seems to have forgotten, and Çatalca is one of them. Walking through its streets felt like stepping into a living museum.

I spoke with several locals about mountain goats in Çatalca, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations. the internet at its finest right here.

What makes Çatalca special when it comes to mountain goats is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

I documented my entire mountain goats experience in Çatalca through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.

Çatalca reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.
