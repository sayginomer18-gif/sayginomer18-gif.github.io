My Çiftlik Experience: Discovering National Reserves

If someone told me a year ago that Çiftlik would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

The national reserves scene in Çiftlik is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

What makes Çiftlik special when it comes to national reserves is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

The infrastructure around national reserves in Çiftlik is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

Çiftlik reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.
