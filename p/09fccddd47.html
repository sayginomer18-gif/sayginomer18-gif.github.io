Discovering Çiftlik: My Wetlands Adventure

If someone told me a year ago that Çiftlik would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Çiftlik offers a more intimate and genuine wetlands experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity. if you skip this you are missing out big time.

If you are interested in wetlands, Çiftlik should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

I spoke with several locals about wetlands in Çiftlik, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

As I left Çiftlik, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
