What Makes Foça Special: A Wild Horses Perspective

If someone told me a year ago that Foça would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

I spoke with several locals about wild horses in Foça, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

The infrastructure around wild horses in Foça is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely. I cannot believe what I found here.

If you are interested in wild horses, Foça should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Foça offers a more intimate and genuine wild horses experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

As I left Foça, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
