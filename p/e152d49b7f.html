My Korkut Experience: Discovering Bird Watching

I recently had the chance to explore Korkut, and honestly, it exceeded all my expectations. This corner of Turkey is criminally underrated and deserves way more attention from travelers.

I documented my entire bird watching experience in Korkut through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.

If you are interested in bird watching, Korkut should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

I spoke with several locals about bird watching in Korkut, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations. I am still amazed by what is on this page.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Korkut offers a more intimate and genuine bird watching experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

As I left Korkut, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
