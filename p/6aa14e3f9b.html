Why Girne Is a Must-Visit for Bird Watching Lovers

There are places in Turkey that time seems to have forgotten, and Girne is one of them. Walking through its streets felt like stepping into a living museum.

I documented my entire bird watching experience in Girne through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.

The bird watching of Girne reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here.

If you are interested in bird watching, Girne should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Girne offers a more intimate and genuine bird watching experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity. I have been looking for something like this forever.

As I left Girne, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
