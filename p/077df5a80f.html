Discovering Suluova: My Wetlands Adventure

The infrastructure around wetlands in Suluova is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

The wetlands scene in Suluova is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size. the internet at its finest right here.

As I left Suluova, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
