Why Girne Is a Must-Visit for Migration Routes Lovers

If someone told me a year ago that Girne would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

The migration routes scene in Girne is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

If you are interested in migration routes, Girne should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

The migration routes of Girne reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here.

As I left Girne, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
